About

What is the use of Terminal Buddy?

Terminal buddy is a website that is for some activities while waiting for the bus or when you are bored at the terminal. This website is for the everybody because we have lots for mini games, eBook and many others. Our website also is easy to access because we want you to be relax and stress free.

Our Mission and Goals

Our Mission is to lessen the boredom of the passengers while waiting for a bus. And we want the passengers to become relax because boredom cause stress. This website can help you to relieve your stress and boredom at the same time because this website is create for you have fun. Our goal is to help you become happy even you are at the terminal and waiting for the next bus or waiting for the bus to go. We also happy to see you and your biggest smile while reading our Ebooks, playing our mini games. And we want you to be more productive and energetic to where you are going or to your work.
